About Me

Traavis with Two AAs.

My name is Traavis – Yes, two AA’s.

I’m proudly a born and raised pacific northwest guy, who’s had the opportunity and good fortune of living and working in no less than 6 counties across Europe, Canada the United States and Central America. Some might call this adventurous and exciting; others might say it was even a little crazy. The one thing I will say about my adventures is they’ve made me much worldlier; and bestowed upon me a deep respect for communication, and forging and maintaining relationships.

In my working world – Luxury’s the word.

I manage and drive wholesale and retail sales. I understand consumers. I live and breathe luxury.

I am a seasoned and experienced manager with diverse experience integrated both vertically and horizontally across the luxury industry value-chain: From territory management to marketing strategy and business insights; from retail management to wholesale operations. I’ve worked in luxury across Canada, the US and Europe, and I have come to develop an innate understanding of what drives the luxury industry. Outside the office I have completed an Honours Bachelor of Arts, and more recently a global MBA in Marketing and Strategy from two of Europe’s top ranked business schools.

Energizer Brands

London, United Kingdom | Marketing Strategy

Energizer Brands hired me to work on its marketing strategy. The company had experienced exponential growth worldwide, but had not consolidated a base in any of its national or international markets. I was hired by the company as a temporary consultant to help the company identify, target and ultimately begin to develop a local base within the UK market from which it could begin to consolidate and sustain growth. My project identified ways to further regional growth in the UK market by targeting key stakeholders, interest groups and influencers. It further suggested ways to tie together fragmented pieces and concepts henceforth developed within the region, and to consolidate these into the new actionable plan for regional based growth and development.
